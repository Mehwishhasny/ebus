About Us

Our Journey

The amazing mind behind this project is, John Frank Das; who first thought to have a vehicle through which pupil can travel easily and make their bookings hassle-free by just clicking a button. He then shared his idea with his friend, Mr. Rocky Ben; to which the response was great. Then finally the first ebus bus was introduced in 2015by both the friends and number of people have travelled with it. People find it convenient and easy to make their bookings and travel from one place to another. Now we have more than 20 vehicles which served 24-hours of the day and cover many of the routes of the city.
